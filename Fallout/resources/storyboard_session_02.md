## IMAGE 10 — Épilogue : Vieux Colosse devant la muraille ouverte

**Scène :** Matin doré. Vieux Colosse seul, une paume posée à plat sur les pierres de la muraille nord. Le passage qu'il a ouvert est toujours là — il ne l'a pas rebouché. De l'autre côté, le Wasteland s'étend. Derrière lui, New Eden est calme, diminuée, mais vivante. Le G.E.C.K. verdoie toujours.

**Prompt :**
```
Golden morning light on the edge of a post-apocalyptic settlement,

a massive bald heavily mutated humanoid with olive-green leathery deeply furrowed skin, enormous muscular build, wearing simple wrapped earth-tone cloth robes, standing alone before a hand-built stone wall, one huge palm pressed flat against the rough stones,

a gap in the wall — stones carefully removed and stacked to the side, wide enough for a person to pass through crouched, through the gap: the vast empty wasteland stretches to the horizon under golden dawn light,

behind him: the settlement is quiet, some buildings with open doors, lush impossible green vegetation still growing between cracked walls, a technological device glowing faintly in the distance — still working, still sustaining life,

his expression is not sad, not angry — something older than both, contemplation that spans centuries, looking at a wall he built with his own hands and a hole he made with those same hands,

solitude and purpose, a guardian who learned that walls can be cages,

cinematic digital oil painting, retro-futuristic 1950s-inspired post-apocalyptic aesthetic, painterly brushwork with visible texture, dramatic cinematic lighting, muted earthy palette dominated by amber rust and ochre, highly detailed character faces and textures, movie concept art quality, landscape format 16:9
```
